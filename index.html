About Us

Hello guys We are the players of UBA, UBA stand for Uravakonda Basketball Association.And we are here to teach basketball for children age upto 4 years to 16 years. Day one starts with basics like dribbling, passing, shooting and further tips and tricks for flexibility,mobility and fitness. Every day the session starts with streching after streching running means jagging after that starts 100 metre sprint and moves to exercise after that starts German drill.After completion of these things we moved into layup shots, defence shuffle, two man pass, types of crossovers and finally ends with warm down.


About Summer Coaching Camp

In the summer holidays, we started summer camps for children's to improve basketball skills. All the seniors of our UBA forms a group and started announcing about free camp information in school and colleges near us. Camp starts grandly with opening ceremony, And some of our senior players and children parents are come forward to make sponsors for UBA. We are always thanking them for their love for us. At the end of camp, we conducted competitions between children's and provided rewards like momentums,cups, medals and certificates for all the participants.This reward distribution takes place at the end of camp by the name of closing ceremony. There are lots of activities are performed like cultural activities, camp fire and other further speeches from successful person in our areas to gave a positive opinion on sports and games.At the Starting of summer camp we saw less children in ground but day by day the count of children's were increasing and positive support from the children parents.UBA provides some equipments like skipping ropes,balls,cones,rope ladders etc.. Some of our children who are learned form us they are participating in states and nationals team.
